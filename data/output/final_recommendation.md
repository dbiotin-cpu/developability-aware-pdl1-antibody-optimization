# Final Variant Recommendation

## Summary

Developability-optimized variants were ranked by combining pre-structure developability improvement with Boltz-based structure/interface retention metrics.

The final integrated score used four components:

- Developability improvement relative to the parent candidate
- Epitope/contact retention relative to the WT/reference complex
- Interface contact preservation
- Mutation burden

Boltz/RF2-style structural scores are treated as in silico triage, not experimental validation.

## Recommended Variants to Advance

No variant met the strict Advance threshold. Review Backup candidates for possible experimental follow-up.

## Recommended Experimental Validation

Top-ranked variants should be tested experimentally by:

- Transient mammalian expression
- Protein A or affinity purification
- SEC purity and aggregation analysis
- DSF or nanoDSF thermal stability
- SPR/BLI binding to PD-L1
- Cell-surface PD-L1 binding by flow cytometry
- Specificity or polyspecificity assessment
